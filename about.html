About Me

Neuroscientist. Sometimes.

An aspiring neuroscientist studying at Georgia Tech interested in learning more about regenerating human nerves and the spinal cord through the usage of neural stem cells, as well as, using technology to replace nerves and portions of the spinal cord.

Art. Art. Technology.

Although going to college for neuroscience, I love art and how it can be combined with technology to create amazing things that help the community. I have been and always will be first and foremost an artist.

Hobbies.

Loves to bake, watching the stars, making stickers, rewatching Grey's Anatomy and Criminal Minds, organizing everything, and writing, as well as, mentoring high school VEX robotics teams on their engineering logbooks.

Travel.

I try to travel as much as possible, because I love learning more about other cultures and languages. My favorite thing to do when I go somewhere new is trying the local food and sugary drinks. Out of the places that I have been to my favorites are Osaka, Japan; Jeju Island, Korea; San Diego, CA!
