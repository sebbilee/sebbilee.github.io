Sebbi Lee

Neuroscientist. Student. Artist.

An aspiring neuroscientist studying at Georgia Tech interested in learning more about regenerating human nerves and the spinal cord through the usage of neural stem cells, as well as, using technology to replace nerves and portions of the spinal cord.

Current Work.

Working remotely on peripheral nerve injury research at the Cope Lab (located at Georgia Tech), and reports directly to Dr. Travis Rotterman and Dr. Timothy Cope.

Art. Design.

I've been doing art for as long as I could hold a pencil to paper and fell in love with telling stories with them. Ever since, I've been creating things from paintings to Minecraft bees to stickers!

Hobbies.

Loves to bake, watching the stars, making stickers, rewatching Grey's Anatomy and Criminal Minds.
